About the Temple

Sri Shiva Temple, Thatiparthi is a serene abode dedicated to Lord Shiva. Daily poojas and special celebrations like Maha Shivratri and Pradosham are conducted with devotion. This site shares timings, events, photos, committee information and a transparent donations board.

  • Daily Aarti: 6:00 AM & 7:00 PM
  • Open Hours: 5:30 AM – 11:30 AM & 5:30 PM – 9:00 PM
  • Special Days: Mondays, Pradosham, Shravan

Darshan Hours

  • Morning: 5:30 AM – 11:30 AM
  • Evening: 5:30 PM – 9:00 PM
  • Festivals: Extended hours

Pooja Schedule

  • Alankaram & Archana – 10:00 AM
  • Sandhya Aarti – 7:00 PM
